# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1. All contributors, maintainers, and participants in project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and any associated communication channels) are expected to abide by it.

## Scope

This Code applies to all project spaces and to public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

## Reporting

Concerns may be reported privately to the maintainer at **okekeag@gmail.com**. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially. The maintainer will determine the appropriate response, which may include a private warning, a public clarification, a temporary suspension from project spaces, or a permanent ban, depending on severity and intent.

## Enforcement principles

Decisions follow the principles in the Contributor Covenant: be welcoming, respect differing viewpoints, accept constructive feedback, and prioritize what is best for the broader research community that depends on this code.
